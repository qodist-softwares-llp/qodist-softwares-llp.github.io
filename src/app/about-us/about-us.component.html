How Our Team Can Help

AI Video Interviews

Candidates complete structured video interviews on their own schedule. The platform records, transcribes, and analyses each answer automatically - no scheduling, no interviewer bias.

LLM-Based Evaluation

Each answer is evaluated by a large language model for content quality, relevance, and communication clarity. Per-question scores and an overall candidate assessment are generated instantly.

Privacy-First by Design

Built for compliance with India's DPDP Act 2023 and GDPR. Candidate consent is collected explicitly before every interview. Video data is retained for 90 days; analysis reports for 1 year.

We Build AI-Powered Products for Modern Hiring Teams.

Rapt Hire is our AI-powered interview platform that helps hiring teams conduct structured video interviews at scale. Candidates complete interviews on their own time; our platform handles recording, transcription, facial verification, and LLM-based evaluation automatically.


Every answer is scored for content quality, communication clarity, and relevance. Integrity signals - face presence, identity verification, focus detection - are surfaced as review flags for HR teams, not automated verdicts. Hiring decisions stay with humans.
