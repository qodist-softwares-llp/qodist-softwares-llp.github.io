About Us

Building AI Products for the Future of Hiring.

Our Principles

Core Values Driving Our Products

Compliance & Trust

We design our hiring tools to strictly align with India's DPDP Act 2023 and GDPR. Candidate data is processed securely, with explicit consent, and automatically purged after standard retention periods.

Fairness & Bias Reduction

By structuring every interview and evaluating answers against objective rubrics, we help eliminate human interviewer bias and promote equal opportunity.

Human-in-the-Loop AI

We believe AI is a powerful assistant, not a final decision-maker. Our scores and behavioral flags act as structured recommendations, ensuring final hiring decisions remain human.

Innovative

We Build AI-Powered Products for Modern Hiring Teams.

Rapt Hire is our AI-powered interview platform that helps hiring teams conduct structured video interviews at scale. Candidates complete interviews on their own time; our platform handles recording, transcription, facial verification, and LLM-based evaluation automatically.


Every answer is scored for content quality, communication clarity, and relevance. Integrity signals - face presence, identity verification, focus detection - are surfaced as review flags for HR teams, not automated verdicts. Hiring decisions stay with humans.
